Cyber Security

An Intrusion Detection System (IDS) monitors networks for malicious activity or policy violations, with signature-based IDS detecting known attack patterns and anomaly-based IDS identifying unknown attacks. However, signature-based IDS struggle with new attacks, while anomaly-based IDS can be time-consuming. To address these issues, we propose an automated approach to extract and evaluate attack signatures, improving detection efficiency. Our project employs a feature selection mechanism using recursive feature elimination and a decision tree-based classifier to enhance speed and accuracy by focusing on relevant features.
